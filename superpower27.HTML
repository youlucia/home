My Superpower

What is it ?

My superpower is control water. So that means I can separate different debris from water, I also can make a bubble full of air or gathered air in water so I can stay in water for a long time, I don’t need to worry about water pressure so I can dive very deep. I don’t need to swim, I just need to control water to push me.

How I get it ?

One day I drink some unknown liquid, when I realize something goes wrong, it was too late. After that I sick for days, and I found I got the power of control water. I never tell this to anyone yet, I know my power is dangerous. So I move to an island.

What I do with it ?

Now I live near a beach, save people who is drowning. I don't want others know I have superpower because I know it will course me trouble. I like my power but it completely change my life. Now I hide my power and live like others, but I never stop worry about if others know my secret.
